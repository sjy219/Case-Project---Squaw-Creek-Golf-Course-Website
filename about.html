SQUAW CREEK GOLF COURSE

ABOUT US

  Squaw Creek Golf Course, named for the winding creek that comes into play on 12 of the 18 holes of this stunning design, is located in Willow Park, Texas, an easy 20 minute drive from downtown Fort Worth.

Don’t let the yardage, somewhat short by today’s standards at 6958 yards, fool you. Squaw Creek’s pecan tree lined fairways, beautiful 30 acre lake, winding creek, surprising elevation changes and tricky undulating greens offer plenty of challenges for the scratch golfer while being extremely enjoyable for all skill levels.

Squaw Creek has hosted many Amateur, Collegiate, and Professional events including the PGA Tour Caddis Association Championship on four occasions.

Named by the Dallas Morning News as “best bang for the buck” in Dallas/Fort Worth, Squaw Creek is the perfect place to call home for your next golf outing.

Why Squaw Creek for your next outing? Besides a beautiful course, competitive rates, fully stocked pro shop, and a top of the line driving range and short game facility, Squaw Creek allows you to bring your own food and beverages, including alcohol.

Need more information? Please call our pro shop at 817-441-8185 and let our experienced staff schedule your next event.

BEGINNER FRIENDLY

 For a beginner, golf can be too competitive, stuffy, and very intimidating. There are way too many complicated rules and expectations. Relax. There may be a time when you want to keep score, know all the rules, or play in a tournament, but until that time comes, try these suggestions:

    Don’t keep score.

    Play from the short tees.

    Only count shots when you hit the ball.

    Play in your tennis shoes; be comfortable.

    Move your ball away from trees and rocks.

    Skip a hole if you need a break.

    Just chip and putt if you want to.
